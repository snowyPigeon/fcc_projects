A Tribute to Steffi Graf

Tennis Career

Steffi Graf is a former professional tennis player who was a dominant figure during the 1980s and 90s. Graf won her first grand slam or major tennis tournament in 1987 at the French Open. In 1988, Graf achieved the Golden Slam winning all four major tournaments and gold at the Olympics in Seoul. The grand slams included the Australian, French, US and Wimbledon Open tournaments.

Graf continued her successful career through the 1990s. Graf retired in 1999 following injuries and a defeat at Wimbledon.
